QUESTION:

Write a program to calculate the square and cube of numbers 2, 3, and 4.

CODE:

def calculate_square_and_cube(number):
    square = number ** 2
    cube = number ** 3
    print(square)
    print(cube)


calculate_square_and_cube(2)
calculate_square_and_cube(3)
calculate_square_and_cube(4)


OUTPUT:

8
9
27
16
